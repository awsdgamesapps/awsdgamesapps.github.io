Privacy Policy

TapRiot - Update: April 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

The TapRiot application collects certain data to provide league features and improve your gaming experience. This includes a username or player identifier, tap scores and game results, league ranking data, and usage data to display ads through Google AdMob.

2. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect and use data (such as device identifiers and usage data) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, as described in their privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

3. League and Game Data

To enable league functionality, we collect and store player scores, rankings, and in-game identifiers on our servers. This data is used exclusively to display leaderboards and manage competitive leagues. We do not share this data with third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

4. Data Retention

Player data associated with leagues is retained for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

6. Contact

Email: privacy@awsdgamesapps.com

Delete TapRiot Account

Account and personal data deletion request

What data is deleted?

Deleting your account permanently removes the following data:

  • Your username
  • Your score history and game results
  • Your league history
  • Your friends list
  • Your coins and in-app purchases

What data is retained?

Anonymized service usage data may be retained for up to 90 days for security and fraud prevention purposes.

How to request deletion

To delete your account and all your data, send an email to:

privacy@awsdgamesapps.com

Use the subject line: "Delete TapRiot Account" and include your username in the message body. We will process your request within 30 days.

In-app deletion

You can also delete your account directly from the app at: Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
